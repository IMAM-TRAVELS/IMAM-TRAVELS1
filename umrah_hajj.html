Umrah & Hajj FAQs

Umrah can be performed year-round. However, Rajab, Shaaban, and Ramadan are considered excellent months. Winter months (November-January) are also comfortable due to cooler weather.

The rituals of Umrah itself take 2-3 hours. However, most packages are 7-10 days to allow for multiple Umrahs, rest, and visits to other holy sites.

Women can perform Umrah with or without a Mahram (male guardian). However, for group packages, we recommend traveling with family or joining organized group tours for safety.

Hajj package costs range from PKR 399,900 to PKR 899,900 depending on accommodation quality and services included. Visa quota and limited seats affect availability.

All our packages include visa processing, accommodation, meals, ground transportation, airport transfers, guides, and 24/7 support.
